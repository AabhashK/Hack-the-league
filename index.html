About Our Vision

We believe that technology when meeting the right opportunity has the power to create a real impact on major human problems. We aim to empower students to utilize the best of their innovative abilities in idea generation, development, and design to develop applications that have an unimaginable impact on the world. Whatever the focus may be but we want to make an impact-driven community through students, tech, and innovation!



Tracks

Our tracks focus on real world problems and we intend that teams will come up with innovative projects to solve them with the help of technology! Different themes denote different zones of problems and you can create a project to solve any problem in that zone!
